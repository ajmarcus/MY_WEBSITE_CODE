CAST

CAST, my MFA exhibition completed in 2015, explored the layering of paint and light in such a way that the individuality of each layer was obscured. Overhead projectors threw painted transparencies upon 8’ x 11’ painted paper backdrops. It was unclear where the actual painting ended and the projection began. Neon lights were programed to fade on and off at an almost imperceptibly slow rate. CAST was an environment composed of complex visual stimuli changing slowly over time.
